<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Natural Rubber Contact Adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based adhesives and waterborne adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents for effective bonding and are valued for their strong initial tack and quick drying times, making them popular in construction and automotive applications. In contrast, waterborne adhesives are more environmentally friendly, using water as a solvent, and are preferred in industries seeking lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions while still providing good adhesion and flexibility.</p></p>
