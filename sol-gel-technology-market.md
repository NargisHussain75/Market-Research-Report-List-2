<p><p>Sol-gel technology is a versatile process used to produce materials through the transition of a solution into a solid gel phase. The market is primarily divided into two types: coatings and deposition. Coatings involve applying a sol-gel solution to surfaces to create thin, protective films with desirable properties like corrosion resistance and thermal stability. Deposition, on the other hand, focuses on layering materials onto substrates, often enhancing functionality in electronics and optics, enabling advanced applications in various industries.</p></p>
