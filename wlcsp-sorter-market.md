<p><p>The WLCSP (Wafer Level Chip Scale Package) sorter market encompasses three primary types: wafer sorters, die sorters, and test sorters. Wafer sorters handle wafers at the initial stage, organizing them post-fabrication based on electrical tests. Die sorters focus on sorting individual dies from diced wafers, ensuring quality and efficiency in packaging. Test sorters evaluate the performance of packaged devices, verifying functionality and reliability. Together, these sorting systems enhance production efficiency and yield in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
