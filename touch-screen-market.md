<p><ul><li>Resistive Film Touch Panels</li><li>Capacitive Touch Panels</li><li>Projected Capacitive Touch Panels</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Touch Panels</li><li>Optical Touch Panels (Infrared Optical Imaging Touch Panels)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen market features various technologies catering to different applications. Resistive film touch panels sense pressure, making them cost-effective for many devices. Capacitive touch panels utilize the electrical properties of the human body for a more responsive experience. Projected capacitive touch panels enhance multi-touch capabilities for advanced interfaces. Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) panels offer high image clarity and durability through ultrasound waves. Optical touch panels use infrared technology for precise touch detection, ideal for interactive displays.</p></p>
