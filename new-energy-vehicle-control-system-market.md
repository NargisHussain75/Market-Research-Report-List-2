<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Control System market includes two primary components: the Vehicle Control Unit (VCU) and the Motor Control Unit (MCU). The VCU manages overall vehicle functions, integrating various subsystems for optimal performance, safety, and energy efficiency. In contrast, the MCU specifically controls the electric motor’s performance, regulating speed, torque, and energy consumption. Together, these units enhance the functionality and efficiency of NEVs, contributing to the growing demand for sustainable transportation solutions.</p></p>
