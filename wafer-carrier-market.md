<p><p>The wafer carrier market primarily consists of two types: wafer cassettes and wafer frames. Wafer cassettes are enclosed containers designed to hold multiple wafers securely during transport and storage, ensuring protection from contamination and mechanical damage. In contrast, wafer frames are open structures that support individual wafers, allowing for optimal access and handling during processing and inspection. Both types play crucial roles in the semiconductor manufacturing supply chain, enhancing efficiency and safeguarding wafer integrity.</p></p>
