<p><p>The organ transplant rejection medicine market comprises various types of immunosuppressants aimed at preventing organ rejection. Steroids reduce inflammation and suppress immune responses. Antibodies, such as monoclonal antibodies, target specific immune cells to decrease rejection rates. mTOR inhibitors block cell division, inhibiting T-cell activation. Antiproliferative agents disrupt the proliferation of lymphocytes, reducing immune responses. Calcineurin inhibitors prevent T-cell activation by inhibiting key proteins, crucial for immune response. Together, these treatments enhance transplant success and patient outcomes.</p></p>
