<p><p>The floating solar panels market is primarily divided into two types: solar-tracking and stationary floating solar panels. Solar-tracking floating solar panels utilize advanced mechanisms to follow the sun's trajectory, optimizing energy capture throughout the day. This technology can significantly enhance efficiency and yield. In contrast, stationary floating solar panels are fixed in position and designed for less complex applications, often suitable for large water bodies. Both types contribute to sustainable energy solutions while minimizing land use and harnessing otherwise unused water surfaces.</p></p>
