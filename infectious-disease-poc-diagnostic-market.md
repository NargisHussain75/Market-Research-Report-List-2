<p><p>The infectious disease point-of-care (POC) diagnostic market encompasses various testing methods. Lateral flow immunoassays provide rapid, visual results for specific antigens or antibodies. Agglutination tests detect pathogen presence through visible clumping reactions. Flow-through tests offer quick results by allowing samples to pass through a reagent medium. Molecular diagnostics utilize techniques like PCR to identify genetic material from pathogens, delivering precise results. Other methods may include tissue culture or biosensors, broadening the diagnostic landscape for infectious diseases.</p></p>
