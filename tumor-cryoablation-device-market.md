<p><strong>The Tumor Cryoablation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phase Change Refrigeration</li><li>Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tumor Cryoablation Device market primarily features two refrigeration types: Phase Change Refrigeration and Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration. Phase Change Refrigeration utilizes a refrigerant that absorbs heat during phase transition, efficiently lowering temperatures to destroy tumor cells. In contrast, Joule-Thomson Throttling Refrigeration relies on the Joule-Thomson effect, where gas expansion cools the device, allowing precise temperature control. Both technologies facilitate targeted ablation, offering effective treatment options for various tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
