<p><p>The Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) test strips market is segmented into three main types: White Blood Cell (WBC) test strips, nitrite test strips, and other tests. WBC test strips detect the presence of white blood cells in urine, indicating inflammation or infection. Nitrite test strips identify the presence of nitrites, which are produced by certain bacteria associated with UTIs. Other tests may include pH level assessments or specific gravity measurements, providing additional information for diagnosing urinary tract infections.</p></p>
