<p><ul><li>Torrefaction</li><li>Steam Explosion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced Wood Pellets market encompasses various types, primarily torrefaction and steam explosion processes. Torrefaction involves heating biomass in a low-oxygen environment, enhancing energy density and making pellets easier to grind and transport. In contrast, steam explosion utilizes high-pressure steam to break down lignocellulosic materials rapidly, improving accessibility for fermentation and conversion into biofuels. Both methods enhance the quality and efficiency of wood pellets, making them more competitive in renewable energy markets while promoting sustainability.</p></p>
