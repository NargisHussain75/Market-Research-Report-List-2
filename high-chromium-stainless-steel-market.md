<p><p>The high chromium stainless steel market is divided into four main types: </p><p>1. **Ferritic Stainless Steel** offers good corrosion resistance and is magnetic, used mainly in automotive and industrial applications.</p><p>2. **Martensitic Stainless Steel** provides high strength and hardness, suitable for tools and blades.</p><p>3. **Austenitic Stainless Steel** is non-magnetic and highly versatile, ideal for kitchenware and chemical processing.</p><p>4. **Precipitation Hardened Stainless Steel** combines high strength with corrosion resistance, commonly used in aerospace and military applications.</p></p>
