<p><strong>The Micro Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Micro Pump</li><li>Non-Mechanical Micro Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro pump market is divided into two main types: mechanical and non-mechanical micro pumps. Mechanical micro pumps utilize moving parts, such as pistons or diaphragms, to control fluid movement, offering precision and reliability for applications like medical devices and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, non-mechanical micro pumps operate without moving components, often using methods like electrokinetic or electromagnetic forces, making them more compact and less prone to wear. Both types are essential for advancing microfluidic technologies across various industries.</p></p>
