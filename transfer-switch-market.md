<p><p>The transfer switch market is categorized mainly into automatic and manual types. Automatic transfer switches (ATS) seamlessly detect power outages and switch to backup generators without human intervention, ensuring continuous power supply, which is crucial for critical operations. In contrast, manual transfer switches require users to switch power sources manually during an outage, offering a more economical solution. Both types cater to different needs, with ATS favored for reliability and automation, while manual switches appeal to budget-conscious customers.</p></p>
