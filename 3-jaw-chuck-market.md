<p><p>The 3 Jaw Chuck market can be categorized into three distinct types based on mounting configurations: Front Mount, Rear Mount, and Both Front and Rear. Front Mount chucks attach to the front of the spindle, allowing easy workpiece loading and quick setup, ideal for precision machining. Rear Mount chucks are mounted behind the spindle, offering stability for larger, heavier workpieces. The Both Front and Rear type combines both configurations, providing versatility for various applications and enhancing productivity across different machining setups.</p></p>
