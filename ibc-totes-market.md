<p><p>The IBC totes market includes two primary types: HDPE IBC and Composite IBC. HDPE IBCs are made from high-density polyethylene, offering excellent chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for various liquids, including hazardous materials. In contrast, Composite IBCs combine an inner container made from plastic with an outer frame of steel or a similar material, providing enhanced strength and protection. Both types are widely used in industries such as chemicals, food, and pharmaceuticals for efficient storage and transportation.</p></p>
