<p><p>The Variable Conductance Heat Pipes market can be categorized into two main types: Wicked with Cold Reservoir and Wickless with Hot Reservoir. Wicked heat pipes utilize a porous structure to transport working fluid, where the cold reservoir enhances condensation. This design is effective for managing heat in varied applications. Wickless heat pipes, on the other hand, rely on gravity and thermal gradients, using a hot reservoir to facilitate efficient heat transfer. Both types offer unique benefits for temperature regulation in various industries.</p></p>
