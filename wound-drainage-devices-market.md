<p><ul><li>Passive Drains</li><li>Active Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wound drainage devices market is primarily categorized into passive and active drains. Passive drains rely on gravity and natural drainage to remove fluids from a wound, typically involving a simple tube system that allows fluid to exit the body. In contrast, active drains use suction to actively draw fluid out, enhancing drainage efficiency. Both types are crucial in managing postoperative wounds, reducing infection risk, and promoting healing by preventing fluid accumulation, thereby improving patient outcomes in surgical care settings.</p></p>
