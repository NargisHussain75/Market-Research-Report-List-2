<p><p>The disposable medical latex gloves market is divided into powdered and non-powdered types. Powdered latex gloves have a dusting of cornstarch to aid in donning and removing them easily, providing comfort during prolonged use. However, they may cause allergic reactions in some users. Non-powdered gloves, on the other hand, are treated to enhance slip resistance without the use of powder, making them suitable for sensitive applications and reducing the risk of contamination and allergy concerns in medical settings.</p></p>
