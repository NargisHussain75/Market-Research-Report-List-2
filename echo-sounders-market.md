<p><p>The echo sounders market comprises two primary types: dual frequency and single frequency echo sounders. Dual frequency echo sounders utilize two frequencies simultaneously to provide enhanced depth measurements and better target identification, making them suitable for diverse marine applications. In contrast, single frequency echo sounders operate on a single frequency and are typically used for simpler tasks, such as basic depth gauging. Both types are essential for navigation, fishing, and environmental monitoring in aquatic environments.</p></p>
